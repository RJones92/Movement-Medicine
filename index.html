About Osteopathy

Osteopathy is a system of whole body assessment which indicates the likely injured tissue, shortened or weak muscles, and postural imbalances. Using a variety of tests, observing movement patterns and palpation of your body allows an Osteopath to formulate the best treatment approach for your body and you.

Treatment - Home Visits

Consultation

The initial appointment will be 45 minutes which is comprised of a brief discussion of the problem or concern, I may ask about your medical background as it may be relevant to the issue.

I will then ask for you to undress and expose the problem region of your body (bras and underwear will be kept on).

You will then do some movements and tests whilst I observe in order to establish the injured tissue. Following this I will begin treatment and discuss treatment options available for your problem.

£65

45 minutes

Follow Up

Follow up treatments are 30 minutes where I will ask a few follow up questions to hear your progress, I may retest to see any changes and then we will start your treatment.

During treatment sessions I will use a variety of techniques from stretches, soft tissue to deep tissue massage, joint articulations, manipulations, dry needling and many others.

£50

30 minutes

Preparing for your visit

I will need to be able to park my car within a short walk from your home and we will need enough space in your home for my treatment plinth to be set up and room for me to manoeuvre around during the treatment session.
